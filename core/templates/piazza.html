Exam 2

Exam 2 will take place on December 15th from 8:30PM-10:30PM. The location has not yet been determined and will be announced at a later date. The exam will cover the material from Week 6 until the end of the course, it is not cumulative.

 

You are allowed to bring a double-sided 8.5" x 11" sheet of paper with notes to use during the exam. You should also make sure to bring your student ID with you.

 

To help you study, we have created a study guide:

 

Exam2StudyGuide.pdf

 

A lot of material is also covered in the textbook:

 

Textbook

 

Finally, here is a copy of last year's exam that you can use to practice:

 

CSE132FinalExamS16.pdf

 

For the practice exam, please note that we did not cover the same material that was covered last year, so there may be things on the practice exam that we have not explicitly discussed. Please use common sense when looking at the practice exam and if you have any doubts about what will or will not be covered on our exam refer to the study guide.

 

You are also encouraged to rewatch the videos and ask questions during office hours. In class next Monday we will be having a review session where we will go over some practice problems and answer any questions you may have.

 

Please let us know if you have any questions.
