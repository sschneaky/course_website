HCI Design Project

Everyone will participate in a semester-long HCI design project which will provide an opportunity to use the techniques we cover in class in much greater depth.

The design project consists of several parts:

Project Pitch: Due 9/15
Milestone 1: Due 10/11
Milestone 2: Due 10/30
Milestone 3: Due 11/21
Milestone 4: Due 12/9



Project Pitch

Over the course of the semester, everyone will work on a group project. To select the projects and the groups, everyone in the class will present an idea for a project. Then, we'll select 3-5 of the projects presented as the group projects for the semester based on interest.

For this assignment, you'll need to deliver a 1-2 minute in-class presentation describing your proposed project. The goal of the project pitch is to entice others to vote for your project.
